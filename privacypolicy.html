Our Privacy Commitment

At The Body Factory, we value your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect details such as your name, contact number, email address, and any data submitted through forms (e.g., membership or inquiry forms). Additionally, anonymous data such as browser type and device information may be collected to enhance your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process memberships and respond to inquiries.
  • To improve our gym services and customer experience.
  • To send updates, offers, and fitness-related content (with your consent).
  • To maintain website security and prevent unauthorized access.

3. Data Protection

We implement strict security protocols and encryption methods to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your information is handled only by authorized personnel.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except where legally required or when working with trusted service providers under confidentiality agreements.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can modify your browser settings to disable cookies at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your personal information. You may request correction, deletion, or access to your data by contacting us directly.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect new practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, please contact us at info@thebodyfactory.com
